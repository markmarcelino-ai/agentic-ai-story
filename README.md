# AGENTIC – The Rise of Autonomy

AGENTIC is an AI-themed ebook exploring the evolution of artificial intelligence from simple tools to autonomous agents.

The project examines how humans and AI systems may collaborate in the future and how increasing autonomy could reshape creativity, work, and decision making.

---

## Concept

The book presents a narrative exploration of:

• The evolution of AI systems  
• Human–AI collaboration  
• Autonomous digital agents  
• The future of intelligent systems  

Rather than focusing purely on technical explanations, the book explores these ideas through storytelling and conceptual discussions.

---

## Development

This project is being developed through **human–AI collaboration**.

The writing process involves:

• AI-assisted brainstorming  
• Collaborative writing with AI tools  
• Iterative refinement of ideas and narrative structure  

---

## Current Status

Writing in progress.

Several chapters have already been drafted and the project continues to evolve as new ideas emerge.

## Purpose

The goal of AGENTIC is to explore how AI systems may transition from passive tools into active collaborators in human creativity and problem solving.
